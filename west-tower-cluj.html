Overview

West City Tower is a modern residential landmark, standing as one of the tallest buildings in Romania with 25 floors. It offers stunning panoramic views of Cluj-Napoca and the surrounding areas.

The tower features 1, 2, and 3-bedroom apartments, including luxurious penthouses, all with modern finishes. It is ideal for residents and visitors seeking comfort, privacy, and high-end urban amenities like underground parking and fast elevators.
