Escape Room Cluj (The Ultimate Challenge)

See Gallery

Overview

Escape Room Cluj offers interactive and immersive puzzle experiences designed for groups of friends, families, or colleagues. Participants are "locked" in a themed room and must solve a series of puzzles, riddles, and challenges to escape within 60 minutes.

It promotes teamwork, critical thinking, and fun under time pressure. Whether you want to solve a mystery, break out of a prison, or find a hidden treasure, it's a top choice for leisure and team-building activities.

Opening Hours

  • Monday – Friday 12:00 – 22:00
  • Saturday – Sunday 10:00 – 23:00

*(Booking in advance is highly recommended)*

Price Estimates:

  • Group of 2 ~60 RON / person
  • Group of 3-4 ~50 RON / person
  • Group of 5+ ~40 RON / person
