Overview

Grand Hotel Napoca is a 4-star hotel located near the center of Cluj-Napoca, just about 250m from Central Park and close to the Old Town, attractions, transport, and city amenities.

The hotel has been recently renovated and offers a range of rooms, from standard rooms to apartments and suites. In addition to accommodation, Grand Hotel Napoca provides a restaurant, bar, private parking, and extensive conference facilities, making it suitable for both tourists and business travelers.
