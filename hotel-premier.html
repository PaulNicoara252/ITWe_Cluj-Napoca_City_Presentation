Overview

Hotel Premier is a 4-star hotel located in a quiet area of Cluj-Napoca on Donath Street, just a short distance from the city center. The hotel offers comfortable rooms with modern amenities, including free Wi-Fi, air conditioning, and minibar.

With facilities such as a restaurant, lounge, and conference rooms, it is suitable for both tourists seeking relaxation and business travelers. Its location offers a peaceful retreat while remaining accessible to major attractions.
