Overview

Soviet is a popular themed bar and restaurant in Cluj-Napoca, known for its retro-communist décor, relaxed atmosphere, and quirky ambiance. It’s a friendly spot for casual meals, drinks with friends, and experiencing a fun, vintage-inspired environment filled with old posters, radios, and memorabilia.

Vibe & Experience

The interior is designed to evoke the nostalgia (and irony) of the communist era, with red walls, Lenin busts, and propaganda-style artwork. Despite the theme, the service is modern and friendly, making it a favorite hangout for students and tourists alike.
