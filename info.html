INFORMATION

Useful Tips & Facts

Planning your trip to Cluj-Napoca? Here is everything you need to know about public transport, local rules, emergency numbers, and practical tips to make your stay smooth and enjoyable.

→ INFORMATION

Public Transport

  • CTP Cluj-Napoca Network
  • Tickets via 24pay app or SMS
  • Operates 5:00 AM - 11:00 PM
  • Night buses available (25N)

Fact & Figures

  • Population: ~320,000
  • Currency: Romanian Leu (RON)
  • Language: Romanian
  • Timezone: EET (UTC+2)

Practical Information

  • Emergency Number: 112
  • Country Code: +40
  • Tap water is safe to drink
  • Power sockets: Type F (230V)

Enjoy & Respect Rules

  • Quiet hours: 22:00 - 08:00
  • No smoking in indoor public spaces
  • Respect historical monuments
  • Keep the parks clean

FAQ

  • Is Cluj safe? Yes, very safe.
  • Can I pay by card? Almost everywhere.
  • Is Uber/Bolt available? Yes.
  • Do people speak English? Yes, widely.
