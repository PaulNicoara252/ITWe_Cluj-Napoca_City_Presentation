Frequently Asked Questions

Here you can find answers to the most common questions visitors have about Cluj-Napoca.

1. What is the best time to visit Cluj-Napoca?
The best time is late spring (May–June) and early autumn (September–October) when the weather is pleasant and most festivals take place. Summer is also popular due to music events like Untold.
2. How do I get from Cluj Airport to the city center?
Options include taxi, ride-sharing services (Bolt, Uber), shuttle buses, or car rental. The airport is about 9 km from the city center (~15–20 minutes by car).
3. Is Cluj-Napoca safe for tourists?
Yes, Cluj is generally safe. Standard precautions apply: watch out for pickpockets in crowded areas and avoid poorly lit streets at night.
4. Which languages are spoken in Cluj?
Romanian is the official language. Hungarian is also widely spoken in some neighborhoods, and English is common in tourist areas, restaurants, and shops.
5. How can I pay for things in Cluj?
The currency is Romanian Leu (RON). Most restaurants, shops, and hotels accept credit/debit cards. ATMs are widely available.
6. What public transport options are available?
Buses, trolleybuses, and trams cover the city. Tickets are affordable, can be bought via kiosks, apps, or on board, and should be validated upon boarding.
7. Are there any local customs or etiquette I should know?
Romanians are friendly and polite. Greetings are important, tipping ~10% in restaurants is customary, and it’s polite to use “Bună ziua” (Good day) when entering shops or restaurants.
8. Can I walk around the city center?
Yes, the city center is pedestrian-friendly, with many attractions, cafes, and parks within walking distance.
9. Are there guided tours available?
Yes, walking tours, bike tours, and even Segway tours are available. You can also find themed tours such as history, street art, or food experiences.
10. Where can I find tourist information?
Official tourist information centers are located near Piața Unirii. Most hotels, hostels, and major attractions provide maps and guides, and online resources and apps can also help.
