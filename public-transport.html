Public Transport (CTP)

Overview

Cluj-Napoca has a well-developed public transport network, mainly operated by CTP Cluj-Napoca. It includes buses, trolleybuses, and trams, providing convenient access throughout the city.

Key Lines:

  • Bus & Trolleybus: Extensive network covering all neighborhoods, connecting the city center, train station, and airport.
  • Tram: Lines 101 and 102 connect the industrial zones with the city center and train station.

Tickets & Pricing

Tickets can be purchased at kiosks (ticket offices), automatic machines at stations, or directly via mobile apps like 24Pay or by SMS to 7479.

  • Single Ticket (60 min) 3 – 4 RON
  • 24-Hour Pass 10 – 12 RON
  • Monthly Pass (Standard) 90 – 100 RON
  • Fine (No Ticket) High Penalty!

Operating Hours & Tips

Hours: Most lines run from 05:00 to 23:00. Night buses are available on specific routes (check the CTP website).

Tips for Tourists:

  • Download the Tranzy app for real-time schedules and maps.
  • Always validate your paper ticket immediately upon boarding.
  • Ride-sharing apps like Bolt and Uber are widely available as alternatives.
