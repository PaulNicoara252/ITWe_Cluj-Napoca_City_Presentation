Untold Festival (The World Capital of Night and Magic)

See Gallery

Overview

Untold is the largest music festival in Romania, held annually in Cluj-Napoca, attracting tens of thousands of visitors from around the world. The festival features multiple music genres, including EDM, pop, rock, hip-hop, and international artists, with impressive line-ups each year.

The atmosphere is vibrant and interactive, with spectacular stages, artistic installations, light shows, and additional activities. Untold has become a symbol of Cluj, offering an unforgettable, international festival experience.
